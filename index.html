The History of Computing

From mechanical calculators to quantum computers

Computing has transformed human civilization in less than a century, evolving from room-sized machines to devices that fit in our pockets.

1940s: The Dawn

ENIAC and the first electronic computers emerge during World War II.

1970s: Personal Revolution

The microprocessor brings computing into homes and offices.

1990s: The Internet Age

The World Wide Web connects the world's computers.

2000s: Mobile & Cloud

Smartphones and cloud computing make technology ubiquitous.

2020s: AI Era

Artificial intelligence reshapes what computers can do.
